1. Introduction

Welcome to Sehati Bethaka ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health-related data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, process, use, and store your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By registering for or using Sehati Bethaka, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any aspect of this policy, please do not use the application.

Effective Date

Last updated: June 23, 2026. This policy is periodically updated to reflect new app versions, features, and security protocols.

2. Information We Collect

To provide high-quality medical tracking, NFC card scanning, and emergency utility features, we collect several categories of information:

  • Personal Profile Data Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Email Address, and Phone Number provided during account setup.
  • Sensitive Medical Data Height, weight, blood type, chronic diseases, medical reminders, and family health histories.
  • NFC Card Data Read/write details for your emergency NFC medical card, such as blood type, drug allergies, and key contacts.
  • Step Tracking Sensor and motion data (Pedometer/Activity permission) to count steps and help track physical fitness.
  • Location Data Precise geographical location when finding nearby hospitals, clinics, or emergency support services.
  • AI Assistant History Diagnostic symptoms, queries, and questions asked to the Sehati Bethaka AI chat advisor.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your data exclusively to deliver and improve our healthcare assistance services. Specifically, we use your information for:

  • Providing personalized medical screening tips, HbA1c schedules, and vaccine alerts.
  • Synchronizing medication schedules and notifications to prevent missed doses.
  • Programming and writing medical info onto NFC emergency health cards.
  • Querying nearest medical institutions based on your real-time GPS coordinates.
  • Interpreting medical questions through the AI Assistant chat system to provide preliminary educational feedback.
  • Optimizing application performance, tracking crashes, and troubleshooting platform bugs.

4. How We Share Your Information

We value your trust. We do not sell, rent, or trade your medical or personal profiles. We only disclose information under the following situations:

  • AI Processing Partners Anonymized symptom details are sent securely to our generative AI infrastructure to provide health advice.
  • Legal Requirements When required to comply with judicial subpoenas, applicable government statutes, or safety procedures.
  • Emergency Scenarios Displaying emergency medical cards or details to responders when scanning your physical NFC card.

5. Data Security

The security of your health data is our absolute priority. We employ state-of-the-art protections to guarantee your information stays private:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All sensitive medical records, profiles, and chats are encrypted in transit using Transport Layer Security (TLS 1.3) and at rest on secure cloud servers.
  • NFC Isolation: NFC medical card profiles are stored directly on your physical hardware chip. You control who scans or modifies this physical data.
  • Access Limits: Administrative access to database systems is strictly isolated, logged, and audited.

6. Freemium Model & Payment Processing

Sehati Bethaka is a freemium application. While the core features (such as emergency numbers, basic NFC logging, and standard medication reminders) are completely free, we will introduce premium features and subscription options in future releases.

Subscription & Billing Terms Secure Financial Transactions

When you purchase premium upgrades, your payment transactions are processed entirely by secure third-party payment gateways (e.g., Apple App Store In-App Purchases, Google Play Billing, or certified card processors). We do **not** store or have direct access to your credit card numbers, billing pins, or banking details on our servers.

7. Your Rights and Data Control

You maintain full ownership and control over your health profile. At any time, you can exercise these options directly in the mobile settings:

  • Access & Export: Retrieve and review the personal information you have entered.
  • Modify: Instantly update profile parameters, heights, weights, or disease history.
  • Delete Account: Permanently wipe your profile and history from our servers using the "Delete Account" button in settings.
